Gaza News

Children are facing severe food deprivation. The Integrated Food Security Phase Classification (IPC) report predicts that renewed military operations, the ongoing full blockade, and the severe shortage of essential life-saving supplies will drive food insecurity, acute malnutrition, and mortality levels beyond famine thresholds over the next five months.

The report, issued by 17 United Nations agencies and non-governmental organizations, indicated that the vast majority of children in Gaza are experiencing severe food deprivation. Rapid increases in acute malnutrition are expected in the governorates of North Gaza, Gaza, and Rafah, alongside limited access to health services and a severe shortage of clean water and sanitation.
